Saudi Econ Coffee Seminar Series

Seminar Series hosted by KAPSARC

Welcome to the Saudi Econ Coffee Seminar Series—a forum for passionate economists and researchers who love exchanging ideas on the latest in economic research. Our goal is to provide a supportive platform for Saudi-based economists to exchange ideas, foster collaboration, and enhance the quality of our research through constructive feedback. Inspired by the U.S. seminar tradition, audience members are encouraged to ask questions throughout the presentation. By rigorously discussing the presented work, we aim to generate new insights for the author, helping them refine their paper and strengthen its potential for publication in an internationally competitive environment.

Mission

We are excited to announce the formation of the Saudi Econ Club, an expansion of the “KAPSARC Econ Junkies” Seminar Series. The club’s core pursuit are economics seminars where researchers, academics, and practitioners come together to rigorously discuss and refine each other’s work. Our mission is to foster a supportive environment for Saudi-based economists to collaborate, exchange feedback, and elevate research standards. By joining the Saudi Econ Club, you become part of a growing network committed to shaping economic thought and policy in the region. Looking ahead, we aspire to secure funding to cover speakers travel costs. We welcome anyone eager to learn, share knowledge, and contribute to the robust economic dialogue in Saudi Arabia.

Format

  • Presentation (40 minutes)
  • Discussion (20 minutes)
  • Location: KAPSARC or MS Teams
  • Time: Wednesday, 4pm
