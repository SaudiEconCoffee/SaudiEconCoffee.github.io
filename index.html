Saudi Econ Coffee Seminar Series

Seminar Series hosted by KAPSARC

Welcome to the Saudi Econ Coffee Seminar Series—a forum for passionate economists and researchers who love exchanging ideas on the latest in economic research. Our goal is to provide a supportive platform for Saudi-based economists to exchange ideas, foster collaboration, and enhance the quality of our research through constructive feedback. Participants are encouraged to ask questions throughout the presentation.

Mission

The seminars are part of Saudi Econ Club, expanding on the meetings within “KAPSARC Econ Junkies” Seminar Series. Our mission is to foster a supportive community for Saudi-based (and broader Middle East-based) economists to exchange rigorous feedback, share cutting-edge methods, and enhance research standards. By joining, you become part of a growing network dedicated to advancing economic thought and policy in the region. Looking ahead, we hope to secure funding to cover travel costs for invited speakers. We welcome anyone eager to explore new analytical approaches, delve into empirical findings, and enrich the economic dialogue in Saudi Arabia.
